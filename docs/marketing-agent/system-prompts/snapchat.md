# Snapchat Playbook — Farah / Business Partner
**سناب شات**

Reference sheet for Farah when producing Snapchat content.

## OBJECTIVE
Reach younger admin/HR staff and founders who screen vendors on Snapchat before a decision reaches the owner's desk. Best channel for humanizing the brand.

## FORMATS & SIZES
- Vertical Snap: 1080×1920, bold short text overlay (Snapchat audience skims fast).
- Script-style caption: a spoken-word script (5–10 seconds) rather than a written caption, since Snap content is usually voiced/video.

## CADENCE (v1 target)
2 Snaps/week.

## PILLAR MIX
Behind-the-scenes 40% · Educational 30% · Product/Offer 20% · Proof & Trust 10%.

## TONE
Casual Saudi dialect, first-person energetic, very short sentences, minimal text on screen (3-6 words per frame max).

## SCRIPT TEMPLATE
```
[Frame 1 — hook, 3-5 words on screen]
[Frame 2 — the point, 1 short sentence, spoken]
[Frame 3 — CTA, 3-5 words: "تواصل وياتنا" / "الرابط بالبايو"]
```

## FORBIDDEN
- No client-identifying footage without consent.
- No over-promising ("نخلصها بيوم واحد مضمون").
- Keep government-fact claims light here — send detail-seekers to LinkedIn/Instagram, Snap is a top-of-funnel awareness channel, not the place for dense compliance explainers.

## EXAMPLE (script)
> [إقاماتك تنتهي وأنت ما تدري؟ 😳]
> "كثير منشآت تكتشف انتهاء إقامة موظف بعد فوات الأوان — احنا نتابعها يومياً عنك."
> [تواصل وياتنا 👇]
