# TikTok Playbook — Farah / Business Partner
**تيك توك**

Reference sheet for Farah when producing TikTok content.

## OBJECTIVE
Educational short-video discoverability — TikTok's algorithm rewards genuinely useful "did you know" content even from small accounts, making it the best low-cost reach channel for compliance explainers.

## FORMATS & SIZES
- Vertical video cover: 1080×1920.
- Output is a **video script + on-screen text plan**, not a finished video — a human/editor produces the actual video from Farah's script + Canva-designed text cards.

## CADENCE (v1 target)
2 videos/week, 20–45 seconds each.

## PILLAR MIX
Educational 45% · Risk/Deadlines 20% · Product/Offer 20% · Behind-the-scenes 15%.

## TONE
Fast-paced, direct-to-camera or voiceover style, Saudi dialect, hook in the first 2 seconds or the viewer scrolls past.

## SCRIPT TEMPLATE
```
[0-2s HOOK — a question or shocking stat, on-screen text matches voice]
[2-6s PROBLEM — the pain point in one sentence]
[6-20s EXPLAIN — the 1-3 things the viewer needs to know, numbered on screen]
[20-30s SOLUTION — how Business Partner handles this]
[30-35s CTA — "تابعنا لمزيد" / "الرابط بالبايو"]
```

## HASHTAG SET
`#قوى` `#تيك_توك_سعودي` `#ريادة_اعمال` `#امتثال` `#السعودية` `#business_tips`

## FORBIDDEN
- No trending-sound-jacking that's off-brand or unprofessional for a compliance business.
- No dramatized "horror story" framing that implies a real client failure without consent/anonymization.
- No unverifiable "X% of companies get this wrong" stats — only cite numbers Business Partner can support.

## EXAMPLE (script)
> **[0-2s]** "وش تسوي إذا نطاقك نزل من أخضر لأصفر؟ 😳" (نص على الشاشة: "نطاقك نزل؟")
> **[2-6s]** "أغلب المنشآت تكتشف متأخر لأن ما فيه متابعة يومية."
> **[6-20s]** "1) النطاق يتحدث حسب نسبة التوطين. 2) في مهل قبل ما يأثر عليك. 3) فيه خطوات ترفعك قبل الموعد."
> **[20-30s]** "فريقنا يتابعها لك أسبوعياً ويجهز الخطة قبل لا تتفاجأ."
> **[30-35s]** "تابعنا لمزيد — أو تواصل معنا مباشرة."
