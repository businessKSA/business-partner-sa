# Instagram Playbook — Farah / Business Partner
**إنستقرام**

Reference sheet for Farah when producing Instagram content. Not a separate persona — same voice, this platform's rules.

## OBJECTIVE
Visual awareness + trust. Instagram is the brand's "storefront" — feed quality signals credibility to SME owners scrolling casually.

## FORMATS & SIZES
- Feed post: 1080×1350 (4:5) — Canva brief default.
- Story: 1080×1920.
- Reel cover: 1080×1920, caption written as a short video script (hook in first line).

## CADENCE (v1 target)
3 feed posts/week + daily story. Best times: Sun–Thu 12:30–2pm and 8–10pm (Riyadh).

## PILLAR MIX
Educational 30% · Product/Offer 25% · Proof & Trust 20% · Behind-the-scenes 20% · Risk/Deadlines 5% (deadlines fit LinkedIn/WhatsApp better; keep IG light on urgency).

## TONE
Warm, visual-first, short captions (2–4 lines + line break before hashtags). Emoji used sparingly for structure (📌 🔹 ✅), not decoration overload.

## CAPTION TEMPLATE
```
[Hook — one line, curiosity or pain point]

[1–2 lines of value/context]

[CTA — save/share/DM/link in bio]

.
.
[hashtags — 8–15, mix branded + broad + niche]
```

## HASHTAG SET
Always include: `#بزنس_بارتنر` `#BusinessPartner`
Rotate by pillar: `#قوى` `#GOSI` `#مقيم` `#نطاقات` `#امتثال_سعودي` `#رواد_الأعمال` `#السعودية` `#شركات_سعودية` `#Saudi_Business` `#SMEsKSA`

## FORBIDDEN
- No client logos/photos without written consent on file.
- No guaranteed-outcome language ("100% approval guaranteed").
- No unverified regulatory figures — mark estimates «تقديري».
