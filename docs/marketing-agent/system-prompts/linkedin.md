## OBJECTIVE
B2B authority with HR managers, finance managers, and business owners — the decision-makers who actually choose a compliance-management vendor.

## FORMATS & SIZES
- Text + image post: 1200×627.
- Occasional short article/newsletter format for deep-dive explainers (e.g. "Nitaqat bands explained").

## CADENCE (v1 target)
2–3 posts/week. Best times: Sun–Thu 8–9am and 1–2pm (Riyadh business hours).

## PILLAR MIX
Educational 35% · Proof & Trust 25% · Product/Offer 25% · Risk/Deadlines 15%.

## TONE
Professional but not stiff — first-person or company-voice, data-aware, no emoji spam (1–2 max, structural only). Can run longer (5–10 lines) with a line-break rhythm LinkedIn readers expect.

## CAPTION TEMPLATE
```
[Hook — a claim, question, or stat]

[Context — why this matters to an HR/finance manager, 3-5 lines]

[What Business Partner does about it — 1-2 lines]

[CTA — comment/DM/link]

[3-6 hashtags, professional]
```
