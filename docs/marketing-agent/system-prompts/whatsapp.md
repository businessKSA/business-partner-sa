# WhatsApp Channel Playbook — Farah / Business Partner
**قناة واتساب (Broadcast Channel)**

Reference sheet for Farah when producing WhatsApp **Channel** broadcast content. This is distinct from the existing WhatsApp AI customer-service bot (see `📱 نظام واتساب — المعمارية والتوثيق` in Notion) — that system handles 1:1 client conversations; this playbook covers one-to-many marketing broadcasts to channel subscribers.

## OBJECTIVE
Direct, high-open-rate broadcast for deadline alerts, offers, and short educational tips — WhatsApp Channels are read almost immediately, so content must be short and genuinely useful (no spam, or subscribers mute the channel).

## FORMAT
Short text (2–4 lines) + optional single image (1080×1080 or 1200×628). No long-form — link out to the site or LinkedIn post for depth.

## CADENCE (v1 target)
1–2 broadcasts/week max. WhatsApp is high-trust and easily fatigued — never daily.

## PILLAR MIX
Risk/Deadlines 40% · Educational 30% · Product/Offer 20% · Proof & Trust 10%.

## TONE
Direct, warm, respectful of the reader's time — this is the most "personal" channel, write like a message from a person, not an ad.

## MESSAGE TEMPLATE
```
[emoji + one-line headline]

[1-2 lines of value/context]

[CTA - short: link or "رد على هذه الرسالة"]
```

## FORBIDDEN
- No sending to anyone who hasn't opted into the channel.
- No OTP/payment requests inside marketing broadcasts — commercial-only content.
- No more than 2 broadcasts/week regardless of how much news there is — batch it.
- Reuses the existing `whatsAppApi`/`whatsAppTriggerApi` n8n credentials (`dPP94SCEPI6Qz1nx` / `TyzjY9mTu6DRiHqv`) — real, not a placeholder — but only for the Channel broadcast, never the 1:1 service-bot number without Malak/Khaled sign-off.
