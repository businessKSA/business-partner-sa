How it works

Four steps — from registering your establishment to a daily alert and a ready action pending your approval

1

Subscribe and register your establishment

CR, Qiwa, Muqeem, GOSI, Mudad, ZATCA, licenses — image, PDF or Excel.

2

The agent reads and analyzes

Extracts dates, numbers and statuses automatically and builds your compliance record.

3

Daily monitoring

Calculates days and risks, and alerts you before any expiry or violation via WhatsApp and email.

4

Every action needs your approval

Prepares the renewal/action and shows it to you — nothing government-related runs without your approval.

What does the agent track for you?

  • Certificates and their expiry dates: Zakat, tax, chamber, GOSI, Saudization, wage protection, IBAN.
  • Workforce and residencies: Work permits (Qiwa), residencies (Muqeem), wage protection (Mudad).
  • Nitaqat and Saudization: Your expected band and how many Saudis you need to match or upgrade.
  • Licenses and location: Municipal license (Balady), Civil Defense, a certified lease contract.
  • Foreign investors: Investment license (MISA) and its dates.
  • Alerts: Daily report + an alert before violations and before any expiry.
